Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts and has since grown into an international conference attracting thousands of professionals from across the globe. Each year, TechCon explores the cutting edge of technology, innovation, and industry transformation.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering a community of learners, builders, and leaders. Our mission is to connect brilliant minds, spark innovative ideas, and empower the future of technology through inclusive and inspiring experiences.
